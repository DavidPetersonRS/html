Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

1990

Rockwell Automation was founded in a small garage with a vision to revolutionize automation technology.

2000

Launched the first version of the Retro Encabulator, setting new standards in the industry.

2010

Expanded globally, establishing offices in over 20 countries.

2020

Introduced the Retro Encabulator 2.0 with enhanced features and improved performance.

2024

Continuing innovation with the latest Retro Encabulator models and expanding our product line.
